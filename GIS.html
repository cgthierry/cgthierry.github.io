Below are example GIS projects

The upper left photo shows a 5-meter TIN layered on top of a a 3-dimensional representation of a DEM of Claremont Canyon. Upper right shows the calculated aspect of the same TIN layer pictured on the left. 3-D models were completed in ArcScene.

The left image shows a voter accessibility to voting booths in the city of San Francisco. The right image shows a gradient of accesibility of fire stations to populations within San Francisco.

The upper left image shows the calculated slopes for the city of San Francisco using ArcScene and viewing the layer in ArcGIS. The upper right image shows the total suitability analysis for the hypothetical situation of an escaped koala from the San Francisco Zoo. Green indicates the most likely location of the koala, while red indicates the least likely location. A network analysis was completed on this suitability analysis to determine the most likely location of the koala.

The above GIF shows the development of dams along Bear Creek in the Briones Reservoir. I completed an analysis of the effect of the dam on the downstream creek and its effect on San Pablo Reservoir.
